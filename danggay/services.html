Municipal Government Services

Essential services provided by the Municipality of Roxas to support residents and businesses.

Municipal Services

The Municipal Hall of Roxas provides comprehensive government services to residents, businesses, and visitors. Visit the municipal offices during business hours or contact the appropriate department for assistance.

Business Permits & Licensing

Registration and licensing services for business operations, trade, and commercial activities in the municipality.

Services Include:

  • Business Name Registration
  • Business Permit (Barangay & Municipal)
  • Occupancy Permit
  • Professional License Processing
  • Health Certificates

Processing Time:

2-5 working days depending on requirements

Contact:

Municipal Hall - Business & Licensing Division

Civil Registry & Vital Records

Official records services including birth, marriage, death certificates and related documents.

Available Services:

  • Birth Certificate Issuance
  • Marriage Certificate Services
  • Death Certificate Services
  • Document Authentication
  • Record Corrections

Fee:

Variable depending on service type

Contact:

Civil Registry Office at Municipal Hall

Public Safety & Security Services

Law enforcement, traffic management, and public safety programs administered by the Philippine National Police and Municipal Local Government Units.

Available Services:

  • Police Reports & Incident Recording
  • Traffic Enforcement & Citations
  • Peace & Order Programs
  • Emergency Response Services
  • Community Safety Initiatives

Emergency Contact:

Municipal Police Station - 24-hour operations

Health & Welfare Programs

Services Available:

  • Vaccination & Immunization Programs
  • Maternal & Child Health Services
  • Disease Surveillance & Prevention
  • Health Education Programs
  • Nutritional Support Programs
  • Social Services & Livelihood Assistance
  • Senior Citizen Benefits
  • PWD Support Services

Contact:

Municipal Health Office & Social Services Office
