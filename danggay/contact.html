Frequently Asked Questions

What are the office hours of the Municipal Hall?

Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday: 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Where is the Municipal Hall located?

The Municipal Hall is in the municipality center of Roxas, Oriental Mindoro, ZIP code 5212. You can easily access it from the main highway.

What services are available at the Municipal Hall?

Services include business licensing, vital records, health and welfare programs, agricultural support, public works, and various other municipal services. Contact the appropriate department for specific needs.

How long does processing of documents take?

Processing times vary depending on the service type and document completeness. Most services take 1-5 working days. Call ahead for specific information.

How do I file a complaint or report an issue?

Visit the Municipal Hall during office hours. The administrative staff will direct you to the appropriate office or department that handles your concern.
