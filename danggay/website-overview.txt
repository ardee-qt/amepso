Website Overview - Roxas, Oriental Mindoro Municipality

TARGET AUDIENCE (7 Sentences)
=================================
The primary audience for this website is tourists planning to visit Roxas or use it as a gateway to Boracay Island, as well as 
potential investors and business partners interested in economic opportunities. Secondary audiences include residents seeking municipal 
services and government information, students researching the municipality, and families looking for travel and tourism information. 
Tourists need clear information about ferry schedules to Boracay, attractions like San Rafael Cave, accommodations, and local dining. 
Investors require details about the port facilities, business sectors, economic indicators, and investment opportunities. Residents need 
easy access to government services, office hours, and documentation requirements. The website design prioritizes clear navigation, mobile 
responsiveness, and organized information to serve diverse user needs. All content uses simple language and visual hierarchy to make 
information easily scannable and accessible.

KEY CONTENT HIGHLIGHTS
=================================
1. Strategic Gateway Position - Roxas as access point to Boracay, Romblon, Palawan
2. 20 Barangays - Demonstrates municipal scope and organization
3. Port Infrastructure - Modern facilities supporting trade and tourism
4. Diverse Economy - Fishing, agriculture, shipping, tourism sectors
5. 58,000+ Residents - Growing municipality with economic significance
6. SIPAG Festival - Annual celebration of local livelihood (Jan 25)
7. San Rafael Cave - Major tourist attraction with natural limestone formations
8. Multimodal Companies - Starlite Ferries, Montenegro Shipping Lines operations
9. Business Opportunities - Investment potential across multiple sectors
10. Government Services - Comprehensive municipal services and support

DESIGN STANDARDS
=================================
- Responsive Design: Mobile-first approach (CSS Media Queries)
- No External Frameworks: Pure HTML5 and CSS3 only
- Accessibility: Proper semantic HTML, alt text for images, good contrast
- Navigation: Consistent, intuitive, appearing on all pages
- Visual Hierarchy: Clear distinction between headings, subheadings, body text
- Card Layout: Grid-based card system for organized content presentation
- Image Integration: Placeholder images with descriptive alt text
- Footer: Consistent across all pages with quick links and contact info
